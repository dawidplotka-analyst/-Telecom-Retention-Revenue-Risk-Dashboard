# 📉 Telecom Retention & Revenue Risk Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview
End-to-end Power BI project identifying **revenue at risk** using predictive churn modeling. The dashboard focuses on actionable insights to reduce customer attrition in key financial segments.

---

## 💡 Key Insights
* **80% of revenue loss** is concentrated in just **5 segments**.
* **Critical Window:** The highest churn velocity occurs within the first **22 months**.
* **Contract Risk:** **99.9% of churned clients** were on "Month-to-month" plans.
* **Fiber Optic Trap:** High-revenue Fiber Optic users are the most likely to leave if they lack **Tech Support**.

---

## 📊 Risk Segmentation

| Risk Tier | Potential Loss (per segment) | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🔴 **HIGH Risk** | **$30,000 / mo** | Immediate Retention Offers |
| 🟡 **MEDIUM Risk** | **$70,000 / mo** | Loyalty Programs & Bundling |

### 🔍 Top 5 High-Impact Segments
1. **Month-to-Month + Fiber Optic** (High cost, low commitment)
2. **Fiber Optic + No Tech Support** (High friction)
3. **Electronic Check + No Online Security** (Low trust/integration)
4. **Month-to-Month + No Online Backup** (No "data gravity")
5. **Fiber Optic + No Online Security** (Premium price, low perceived value)
